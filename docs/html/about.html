About AMSIMUN

AMSIMUN is an academic platform for students to debate pressing international issues, develop diplomacy skills, and represent ideas with confidence, structure, and leadership.

Who We Are

AMSI Model United Nations gives students a serious yet inspiring environment where they can research, negotiate, debate, and collaborate on real global matters.

By students, for students, AMSIMUN encourages critical thinking, public speaking, teamwork, and meaningful engagement.

What Participants Gain

Delegates learn how to write resolutions, defend positions, navigate formal procedure, and communicate under pressure. The experience also strengthens confidence, leadership, and awareness of world affairs.
