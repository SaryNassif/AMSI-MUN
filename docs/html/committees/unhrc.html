Committee Chairs

2 chairs assigned to this committee.

Zeina Sulieman

Committee Chair

Zeina Sulieman is currently a Senior who has been a member of the Model United Nations (MUN) since the tenth grade. Having once been in the same position as this year’s delegates, she knows firsthand how every chair, every committee, and every conference can leave an impact in ways one could never imagine. MUN has shaped her into the person she is today. She is excited to see this year’s delegates grow through this experience and hopes they come to love MUN as much as she has. Lastly, she can’t wait to witness the accomplishments of this committee and is certain it will be an unforgettable experience for all involved!

Sidra Al Khatib

Committee Chair

Sidra began her Model United Nations journey in Grade 10. What started as a new experience quickly became something she genuinely enjoyed. Over the past two years, she has served as a delegate in multiple conferences, where she developed her public speaking, critical thinking, and resolution-writing skills. MUN has played a meaningful role in her high school experience, allowing her to grow both academically and personally. She looks forward to continuing this journey and contributing positively to every conference she is part of.
