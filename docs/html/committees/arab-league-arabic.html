Committee Chairs

2 chairs assigned to this committee.

Mohammad Al Falahi

Committee Chair

1. Mohammad AlFalahi is a senior whose MUN journey began in grade 8, when his parents forced him to attend AMSI. After speaking only once and immediately deciding he hated MUN, he left the conference convinced that MUN wasn’t for him. But in grade 11, his growing commitment to school clubs pushed him to give MUN another chance, and that decision changed everything. Since then, he has pushed his way to multiple awards, earning one at almost every conference he’s attended. He believes in enjoying the experience, learning from every committee, and constantly improving his debate skills. Now, Mohammad is a passionate member of MUN, excited for AMSIMUN 2026 and ready to see you all there!

Omar Ayad

Committee Chair

2. It all started in eighth grade when a senior at AMG encouraged Omar to join MUN. At first, he was hesitant. After attending his first ever MUN conference, Junior MUN, he didn’t perform well. That experience made his interest for this club diminish slowly. However, in ninth grade, having discovered the presence of Arabic-speaking committees in MUN conferences, his last spark of interest came back. As a fluent Arabic speaker, his attention was immediately captured. He began participating in Arabic committees in conferences such as AUSMUN and AUDMUN, which helped him gain more experience, build confidence, meet new people, and truly start enjoying the art of debating in Arabic.
